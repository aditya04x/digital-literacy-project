Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist:

- Do not click on suspicious links or emails
- Always check the sender’s email address carefully
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication whenever possible
- Never share OTPs or passwords with anyone
- Always verify payment requests before sending money
- Use only trusted apps for UPI transactions
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Regularly update your devices and applications
- Report cybercrime at https://cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930
